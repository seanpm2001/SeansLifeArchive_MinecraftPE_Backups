Minecraft: Pillager paradise theme

Story so far (As of July 18th 2019)

The pillager army has taken over the world. They plan on killing every last villager as a way of modernizing their existence. They have captured iron golems around the place, burned down and killed all 14 of the first testificates (early villagers) in Minecraft, and have a extermination center in the entrance of their headquarters.
